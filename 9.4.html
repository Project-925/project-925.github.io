        24-Hour Spin Cycle    

    8 AM and this day needs to end, taking
    the 407, the pacing, the trembling,
    random thoughts
    like M134 Minigun rounds, I can’t, I can’t I can’t
    the uni-ball Rollerball pen drops
    out my H&M pocket square and I fall
    apart with it and the bloody ink reflects

    more eyes on me, I don’t even know
    what to think anymore I wish I had my headphones and hat to calm
    me down but instead I’m in a suit with a half-assed tie pondering
    every half second

    had the same experience after the age of 3,
    voice trembled, worried about every word that came out
    worried about every thought other people had
    washed rinsed repeated every single mistake I made
    over and over and over
    wanted words to come out this way, but it came out that way
    worked on it every day, but I went every other way, so I said

    “put one foot in front of the next a fist after the other”
