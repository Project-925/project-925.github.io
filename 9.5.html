        Nature's Prophet    

    leeches in sync, leeches in bleach
    these leeches, they can steal base to base, but can they score runs?

    These colleagues, they ask
    you for directions then run you over, making
    and breaking connections by the hour, plotting
    A slaughter at your feet.

    SO’s don’t freeload.
    Rona rides the tide of your cash flow
    at the speed of a roller coaster, she falls on
    A slaughter at your feet.

    Blood for blood.
    blood brothers don’t talk behind backs,
    if they leech, if they breach,
    A slaughter at your feet.

    Gold diggers only digging up their own graves.
    You give some, you take some,
    but leeches take all.
    Nature slaughters all in a beat.
