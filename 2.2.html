    home          

    glass door

    I am cheap labor
    when people look at my face
    my words are meaningless
    even before I open my mouth

    do you like when I stay quiet?
    do you think I’m invisible when all goes well?
    do you think I’m your punching bag when it goes down?
    is my honesty offensive to you?

    these jokes are hilarious
    especially when you laugh at my dead ancestors
    I am happy when I sit behind the glass door
    because that is best for the team

    I speak great English
    when you are not Caucasian
    I speak great Chinese
    when you are not Taiwanese

     I am extremely easy to be taken advantage of                                                                   
                                                                                by the very people who don’t know me.
