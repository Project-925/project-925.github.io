        Dream Team.    

    A leader is not a boss. A leader listens

    obviously, the goal is comfort
    maybe it really doesn’t matter what you do, you always go
    hard for your team, you want to come
    home feeling like you’ve achieved something, you like
    a diverse team, you play

    no games with colleagues, every now and then, break
    free from your comfort zone, learn, grow, then return
    triumphantly to your inner circle, when your interests become
    commitments, when times get lonely, you want

    something exciting, challenging, when times get busy, things will
    be OK on barbeque Tuesdays, volleyball Fridays, you know
    no OT, you got a team backing you up as repetition leads to change

    obviously, the goal is comfort
    have a night out with your paycheck, and let’s move
    out, have a stable income, save up and pass
    these stacks to progeny, we’ll get by most days, save
    the date for that special occasion

    so what motivates you? why do you do what you do?
