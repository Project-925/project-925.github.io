    this time you thought you’d crack you put one foot in front of the next a fist after the other

    Had first girlfriend at the age of 3. Her name was popular. Needed to see her every day. Thought she was Sexy without knowing what Sexy was.

    Cried as Dad left Mom. Cried saying Bye at YVR. Cried because of slamming doors and screaming. Then, in tears, voice shaking, she whispers: “Do you want to live with Me or Dad?”

    Glued passport to face. Didn’t want to learn Chinese. Wanted to be called Canadian. Didn’t want a Chinese name.

    Maybe some things just don’t change. The Orange rolls from today to tomorrow.

    the words no and no beat within my vague brain
    it comes out real wrong the next thing you know
    the chance has been gone I’m done with collared shirts
    and their fake bot smiles as fake as the plastic you see
    every day these colleagues they leech they breach
    at each opportunity I see from flash to flash
    Bring me Back, Back as Orange rolls by
