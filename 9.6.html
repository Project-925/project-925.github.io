    Zhang Weili    

    Work so hard, is it? Who doesn’t? Work’s a UFC champ, you don’t want to mess
    with work will make you want to fight more, for yourself, for others, work likes
    submission: WorkSafeBC rights as defense, otherwise defenseless.

    You know there’s something so special about work, the memories, a fun reminder of living
    in Two Worlds every weekday, meeting the most amazing colleagues, the more they want to
    break
    you, the more you fight, because work beats you up so bad, but you come

    tomorrow with gashes, mat burns, black eyes still wanting more work can humble
    you, make you a standout no more in the octagon, you can’t ever go
    undefeated in the long run, breakfall and breakfall, you stand
    right back up and continue fighting never breaking, going hard, and challenging
    yourself working each hour you survive

    in the Clock Arena, work doesn’t want you to talk back, you say as I say:
    conflicting opinions quickly resolved
    with BC wildfires. Work so mysterious, a Big Stick in effect from 9 to 5, gone
    right after 5, a revolution in disguise, a daily deception you face.

    “Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life.”

    but some don’t have a choice, some don’t need one, some work 2, some are fighting for one…
